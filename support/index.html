Support and contact

This page is here to help you feel supported during the course. You do not need to figure everything out on your own.

Course support: If you have a question about the course, or you are unsure what to do next, reach out through your WhatsApp group. Your facilitator, your peers, and your fellow Cam Cyntafer(s) are part of the support.
Contact Tidy Butt If you need to reach the organisation directly. Get help now Urgent support and trusted helplines.

If your time or energy is low, focus on the Get Ready step for your next session. That is enough.
