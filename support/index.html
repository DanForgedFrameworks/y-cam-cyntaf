Support and contact

Y Cam Cyntaf is designed to be low-pressure and flexible. If something is not working, or if you feel stuck, please reach out. You are not expected to solve problems alone.

Course support

  • Use your course WhatsApp group to check in with your facilitator and fellow learners.
  • Share questions, successes, or worries. Someone else is probably feeling similar.
  • You can also speak to your facilitator privately during or after live sessions.

Contact Tidy Butt

If you need to talk to Tidy Butt about access needs, safeguarding, or anything that feels bigger than the course, you can reach out directly.
