Welcome to Y Cam Cyntaf

Y Cam Cyntaf means First Steps. This course is about taking small, manageable steps into Welsh, in a way that feels supportive, relaxed, and achievable.

You do not need any prior knowledge. You only need curiosity and a willingness to have a go.

Sessions

Each session is split into four gentle parts:

  • Pre-work – a short warm-up so you arrive confident
  • Live session – relaxed, supportive learning together
  • After the session – a chance to settle what you learned
  • Curiosity Thread – optional, interesting extras

If time or energy is low, just do the pre-work. That is enough.
