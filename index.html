Welcome to Y Cam Cyntaf

A calm, low-pressure introduction to Welsh.

Small steps. Friendly practice. No pressure to be perfect. This course is designed to help you feel more confident with Welsh, while supporting wellbeing and connection.

Use this hub to find what you need for your next session. You can start with Orientation, download print-friendly materials in Downloads, or head to Support and contact if something is not working.
