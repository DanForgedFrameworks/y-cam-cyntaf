Session 6: Bringing it together (and what comes next)

Aim: Bring everything together, celebrate progress, and leave with a simple plan for what you want to do next.

How we’ll achieve this: Friendly recap activities, optional speaking practice, and a supportive “what helped you” conversation.

What we’ll cover: Recap of key phrases, confidence building, and signposting to next steps.

Outcome: You feel proud of what you can do, and you know how to keep going in a way that suits your life.

How to use this page: Use the arrows to move through the steps. If your energy is low, do Get Ready only.

Step 1: Get Ready

Pick three phrases you want to keep:

  • A greeting
  • One “how you are” phrase
  • One useful everyday phrase

Step 2: Get Ready (optional)

Mini reflection: What helped you most in this course?

  • Repetition?
  • Low-pressure practice?
  • Learning with others?

Step 3: Live session

  • Recap activity (supportive and fun)
  • Optional speaking practice
  • Celebrating progress together

Step 4: After the session

Small follow-on: Choose one tiny next step for the next month (for example: one phrase per week).

Pick a curiosity to carry forward:
