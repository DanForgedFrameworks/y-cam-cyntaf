Session 6: Your takeaways and next steps

Session aim: Bring everything together, notice your progress, and decide what your next step looks like.

How we’ll achieve this: We’ll do a gentle recap, celebrate what you can do now, and create a realistic plan to keep going without pressure.

What we’ll cover: review and recap, takeaways, what helped you learn, and how to move beyond your first step.

Outcome: You’ll leave with a clear sense of progress and one realistic next step you can take.

How to use this page: Use the arrows to move through the steps for this session. We understand you are doing this in your own time, so engage with the resources as much as you can. They are here to help you and your peers get the most from the live session. If your time or energy is low, do Get Ready only, so everyone arrives ready. Even if you are not all on the same page, you will be in the same book.

Session steps

Get Ready

Time: 10 minutes.

  • Do a quick recap of your favourite phrases from the course.
  • Choose one phrase you want to keep using after the course.

Live Session

Time: 60 to 90 minutes.

  • Celebrate progress: share a favourite moment or phrase (optional).
  • Gentle recap: what we learned across sessions.
  • Next steps: realistic ways to keep Welsh in your life.

After Session

Time: 5 minutes.

  • Choose one next step you can actually do.
  • Share it in the group if you want, to help you stick to it.

Curiosity Thread

Optional: 5 to 15 minutes.

  • Explore a Welsh word linked to journeys, paths, or moving forward.
  • Pick one that feels meaningful and keep it as your reminder.
