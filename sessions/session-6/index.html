Session 6: Cyntafer Graduation and Learning Styles

Session aim: To consolidate learning, understand personal learning style, and plan next steps.

Key topics: Review, learning styles, next steps

Bloom’s outcome: Evaluate personal learning style and formulate a future learning plan.

How to use this page: Use the arrows to move through the steps. Get Ready is enough if you are short on time.

Session steps

Get Ready

Task: Complete the “Distance Travelled” quiz (compare to Session 1).

Deep Dive: Think about what helped you learn most (listening, repeating, reading, doing).

Live Session

Theme: Graduation Party (60–90 mins)

  • Show and tell: share favourite phrase or moment.
  • Light learning styles discussion: what works for you.
  • Next steps: how to keep Welsh in your life without pressure.

After Session

Task: Download the “Tidy Butt Welsh Resource Pack”. Choose one small next step you can realistically try.

Curiosity Thread

Optional: Welsh words linked to journeys, paths, and moving forward.

Purpose: leave with something meaningful to notice and share.
