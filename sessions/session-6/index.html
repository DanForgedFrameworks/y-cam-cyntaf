Session 6: Bringing it together and next steps

Session aim: Pull together what you have learned and leave with a confident “what next” plan.

How we will achieve this: Friendly recap activities, a few favourite phrases, and a simple next-steps map.

What we will cover: Recap of key phrases, building confidence, keeping Welsh going, and options for further learning.

Outcome: You will leave knowing what you can do next, and with the confidence to keep using Welsh in small, real-life ways.

How to use this page: Use arrows or step buttons. If your time or energy is low, do Get Ready only.

Step 1: Get Ready

Pick one favourite phrase from the whole course. That is your anchor phrase for this session.

Step 2: More Get Ready

Think of one place you might use Welsh next week (a shop, a message, a sign, a friend). No pressure, just a possibility.

Step 3: Live session

We will recap, practise, laugh, and share wins. You will also build a simple plan for keeping Welsh going after the course.

Step 4: After the session

Share one takeaway in WhatsApp. It can be a word, a phrase, or a “I did it” message.

Step 5: Curiosity thread

Pick a Welsh topic you want to explore next (music, nature, mythology, place-names). Share your choice and we will point you to something friendly.
