Session 6: “Cyntafer” Graduation and What’s next

How this session works

Rhythm: Digital pre-work → Live session → Light homework or social application

This is designed to feel low pressure. Small steps count.

Session aim: Bring everything together, celebrate progress, and leave with a simple plan for what you want to do next.

How we’ll do this: Friendly recap activities, optional speaking practice, and a supportive conversation about what helped.

What we’ll cover: Recap of key phrases, confidence building, and next-step signposting.

Outcome (plain English): You feel proud of what you can do, and you know how to keep going in a way that suits your life.

How to use this page: Use Previous and Next to move through the steps. If your energy is low, do Get Ready only.

Step 1: Get Ready

Pick three phrases you want to keep:

  • A greeting.
  • One “how you are” phrase.
  • One useful everyday phrase.

If you have a bit more time: think about which one you want to use in the next week.

Step 2: Live session

  • Recap activity (supportive and fun).
  • Optional speaking practice.
  • Celebrating progress together.

Step 3: After the session

Social: Share a favourite word or moment, or simply attend and listen. Listening counts.

Digital: Download the Tidy Butt Welsh Resource Pack (added in Downloads as it becomes available).

Reassurance

This is a graduation, not a test. You are leaving with tools, not a score.

Step 4: Downloads

We will add a recap handout and next-step signposting here.

Step 5: Curiosity thread (optional)

Pick a curiosity to carry forward:

  • Welsh nature words: choose 5 favourites.
  • Welsh myth or story: learn one name and one place.
  • Welsh heritage: find a local connection and share it.

This is a stretch option. It is for interest and conversation, not performance.

Step 6: End checkpoint (optional)

Take 3 minutes to complete your End of course Distance Travelled check-in. If you saved a Start checkpoint on this device in Session 1 (or Orientation), you will see your distance travelled automatically.

Optional: you can send your scores to Tidy Butt to help evidence course impact for funding and future community delivery.
