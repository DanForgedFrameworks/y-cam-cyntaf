Session 3: Talking about yourself and wellbeing

Session aim: Be able to share how you are doing, and check in with others in a kind way.

How we’ll achieve this: We’ll practise simple “how are you” phrases and gentle responses, with activities that prioritise comfort and choice.

What we’ll cover: how you feel, simple wellbeing check-ins, supportive replies, and listening cues.

Outcome: You’ll be able to say how you are doing and respond to someone else with a supportive phrase.

How to use this page: Use the arrows to move through the steps for this session. We understand you are doing this in your own time, so engage with the resources as much as you can. They are here to help you and your peers get the most from the live session. If your time or energy is low, do Get Ready only, so everyone arrives ready. Even if you are not all on the same page, you will be in the same book.

Session steps

Get Ready

Time: 10 minutes.

  • Watch a short wellbeing check-in clip.
  • Pick one phrase to say how you are, and one phrase to check in with someone.

Live Session

Time: 60 to 90 minutes.

  • Warm-up: short feelings words, no pressure to share personal detail.
  • Pair practice: check in, respond, swap.
  • Group activity: supportive replies and listening cues.

After Session

Time: 5 minutes.

  • Post a short check-in in the group (or reply to someone else).
  • If you prefer, use an emoji plus one word.

Curiosity Thread

Optional: 5 to 15 minutes.

  • Explore a Welsh word linked to feelings, calm, or nature.
  • Share the one you liked most.
