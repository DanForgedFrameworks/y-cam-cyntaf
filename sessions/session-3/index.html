Session 3: Empathy and The Wellness Check-In

Session aim: To discuss personal wellbeing and check in on others in line with the Tidy Butt mission.

Key topics: Wellbeing check-in, listening, supportive phrases

Bloom’s outcome: Describe personal state of mind and demonstrate active listening.

How to use this page: Move through the steps using the arrows. If you are low on energy, do Get Ready only.

Session steps

Get Ready

Watch: “Wellness Check-In” scenario video.

Task: Pick one phrase you can use to check in with someone kindly.

Live Session

Theme: How are you, really? (60–90 mins)

  • Simple wellbeing vocabulary and supportive phrases.
  • Practise asking and answering in low-pressure pairs.
  • Active listening: short responses that show care and attention.

After Session

Task: Post a short check-in message in WhatsApp. If you prefer, respond to someone else rather than starting a new post.

Curiosity Thread

Optional: Signposting to Welsh words connected to feelings, calm, nature, or wellbeing themes.

Purpose: give you something interesting to notice and share.
