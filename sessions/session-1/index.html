Get help now

Session 1

What to do: Do the pre-work before the live session. After the session, complete the follow-up task (5–10 minutes).

If you are short on time, do step 1 only. That is enough to join in.

Pre-work (core)

  1. Watch or read: add your link or PDF here.
  2. Quick check: add your activity link here.
  3. Bring one thing: a question, a word you liked, or a phrase you want to practise.

Live session focus

What we will practise together:

  • Greeting and introductions
  • Simple confidence phrases
  • Short paired practice

After session (homework/social)

5–10 minute task: send one short message in Welsh (or Welsh plus English) using a phrase from today.

This can be a WhatsApp post, a note to yourself, or saying it out loud at home.

Curiosity Thread (optional)

Optional extra: a short link or story connected to Welsh culture, nature, myths, or place names.

  • Signpost to a resource (link)
  • Optional prompt for group chat discussion
  • Optional mini activity (poll or reflection)

Skip this if you want. It is a bonus, not a requirement.
