Session 1: The Foundation and The Social Start

Session aim: To navigate the platform, join the social community, and break the ice with fun colloquialisms.

Key topics: Popti Ping, Hoffi Coffi, Platform and WhatsApp setup

Bloom’s outcome: Identify key colloquialisms and initiate contact in the social group.

How to use this page: Use the arrows to move through Get Ready, Live Session, After Session, and Curiosity Thread. If time or energy is low, just do Get Ready. That is enough.

Session steps

Get Ready

Action: Join the Tidy Butt Learners WhatsApp Group. Task: Post a single emoji or “Bore da” to show you have arrived.

Fun: Watch the “Popti Ping and Hoffi Coffi” video.

Live Session

Theme: Breaking the Ice (60–90 mins)

  • Mentimeter: Live word cloud – “One word that describes how you feel today”.
  • Micro lesson: Popti Ping and Hoffi Coffi, plus a few easy Welsh sounds.
  • Group practice: short repeat-after-me phrases, no pressure to perform.
  • Community: agree WhatsApp norms (kindness, low pressure, consent to respond later).

Aim: lower anxiety and make the first step feel safe.

After Session

Task: Post a voice note or text in the WhatsApp group sharing your favourite “funny” Welsh word (for example Popti Ping). Reply to one other person.

Curiosity Thread

Optional: A short signpost to a Welsh place-name or phrase linked to the local area, plus a quick story behind it.

Purpose: spark interest and conversation, not recall or testing.
