Session 1: First steps and the social start

How to use this page: Use the arrows to move through the steps. If your time or energy is low, do Get Ready only. That is enough to help you and your peers get more from the live session.

Session aim: Get set up, feel welcome, and take your first relaxed step into Welsh.
How we will achieve this: Friendly, low-pressure practice, a few fun phrases, and simple ways to connect without being put on the spot.
What we will cover: Silly Welsh starter phrases, pronunciation basics, and how the group works.
Outcome: You will feel confident enough to say a couple of Welsh words and know how to take part in a way that feels comfortable.
Step

Get Ready

Do the minimum: read the key phrases, check you can access this hub, and bring curiosity, not perfection.

Live session

You will practise together, hear the sounds, try short phrases, and build confidence through friendly repetition.

After the session

Do one small recap. If you want, share a simple Welsh phrase with the group.
