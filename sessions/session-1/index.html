Session 1: First steps and the social start

Session aim: Get set up, feel welcome, and take your first relaxed step into Welsh.
How we will achieve this: Friendly, low-pressure practice, a few fun phrases, and simple ways to connect without being put on the spot.
What we will cover: Silly Welsh starter phrases, pronunciation basics, and how the group works.
Outcome: You will feel confident enough to say a couple of Welsh words and know how to take part in a way that feels comfortable.

How to use this page: Use the arrows to move through the steps. Try to engage as much as you can because it helps you and your peers get more from the live session. If your time or energy is low, do Get Ready only. That is enough.
