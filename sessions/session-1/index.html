Session 1

What to do: Do the pre-work below before the live session. After the session, complete the follow-up task (5–10 minutes).

If you are short on time, do step 1 only. That is enough to join in.

Pre-work (core)

  1. Watch or read: add your link or PDF here.
  2. Quick check: add your activity link here.
  3. Bring one thing: a question, a word you liked, or a phrase you want to practise.
