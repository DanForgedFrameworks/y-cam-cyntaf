Course support

Session 1

This session is about getting comfortable and making Welsh feel doable. Tiny steps, lots of encouragement.

If you only do one thing, do the pre-work. It makes the live session feel easier and more relaxed.

Your session steps

Pre-work

Why it helps: it gives you a head start, so you feel less “on the spot” in the live session.

  1. Read or watch: add your link here.
  2. Quick check: add your activity link here.
  3. Bring one thing: one word or phrase you want to try out.

Aim: 10 minutes. Keep it light.

Live session

Why it helps: this is where confidence builds, because you practise with people, not on your own.

  • Friendly introductions
  • Simple confidence phrases
  • Short paired practice

You do not need to be perfect. You just need to have a go.

After session

Why it helps: a tiny follow-up helps the words stick, so next time feels easier.

5–10 minute task: send one short message using a phrase from today (Welsh or Welsh plus English).

This can be a WhatsApp post, a note to yourself, or saying it out loud at home.

Curiosity Thread

Why it helps: it gives you something interesting to talk about, and helps build connection and wellbeing.

  • A short link to Welsh culture, nature, myths, or place names
  • A simple prompt for group chat discussion
  • An optional quick poll or reflection

Totally optional. Skip it if you are busy.
