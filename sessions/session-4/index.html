Session 4: Getting out and about

Session aim: Talk about getting outside, what you need, and what the world is like around you.

How we’ll achieve this: We’ll use simple scenario practice (weather, kit, plans) and keep it practical so it feels useful in real life.

What we’ll cover: getting out, weather words, basic kit, and simple plans.

Outcome: You’ll be able to talk about a simple plan to get out and about, and what you need for it.

How to use this page: Use the arrows to move through the steps for this session. We understand you are doing this in your own time, so engage with the resources as much as you can. They are here to help you and your peers get the most from the live session. If your time or energy is low, do Get Ready only, so everyone arrives ready. Even if you are not all on the same page, you will be in the same book.

Session steps

Get Ready

Time: 10 minutes.

  • Watch a short clip about weather, kit, and plans.
  • Pick one thing you would take with you, and one weather word.

Live Session

Time: 60 to 90 minutes.

  • Warm-up: weather check-in as a group.
  • Scenario practice: choosing kit and making a simple plan.
  • Friendly group sharing, optional to speak or just listen.

After Session

Time: 5 minutes.

  • Share a simple plan in the group (even one line is fine).
  • Or reply to someone else with encouragement.

Curiosity Thread

Optional: 5 to 15 minutes.

  • Explore a Welsh nature word or place-name.
  • Share one you noticed on a walk or online.
