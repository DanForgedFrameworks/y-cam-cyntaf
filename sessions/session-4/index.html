Session 4: Out and about

How to use this page: Use the arrows to move through the steps. If your time or energy is low, do Get Ready only. That is enough.

Session aim: Use Welsh in everyday situations while you are out in the world.
How we will achieve this: Simple phrases, role-play, and practical scenarios you can imagine using straight away.
What we will cover: Getting out and about, places, directions, and simple everyday interactions.
Outcome: You will feel more confident recognising Welsh around you and using a few phrases in real situations.
Step

Get Ready

Choose one “out and about” phrase and practise it once. That is enough.

Live session

Practise practical scenarios together, with lots of support and repetition.

After the session

Notice Welsh around you this week. If you spot something, share it with the group.

Curiosity thread

Optional: a place name or Welsh sign you can look up and learn from.
