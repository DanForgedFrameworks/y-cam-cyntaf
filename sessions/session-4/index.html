Session 4: Kit, Cold Dips and The Weather

Session aim: To identify the kit for Tidy Butt activities and discuss the weather.

Key topics: Kit, weather, dip versus walk

Bloom’s outcome: Identify necessary items for a specific activity (dip vs walk).

How to use this page: If you only do one thing, do Get Ready so you arrive prepared.

Session steps

Get Ready

Watch: “Dip vs Walk” kit video.

Task: Choose which activity you would do and list 3 items you would bring.

Live Session

Theme: What do we need today? (60–90 mins)

  • Weather words and simple phrases.
  • Kit vocabulary for common Tidy Butt activities.
  • Low-pressure practice: choose kit for a scenario.

After Session

Task: Post a quick kit list in WhatsApp for your next activity. If you prefer, reply to someone else’s post.

Curiosity Thread

Optional: Welsh weather sayings or nature-linked words you might hear in Wales.

Purpose: something interesting to notice and chat about.
