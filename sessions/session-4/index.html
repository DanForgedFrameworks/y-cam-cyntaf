Session 4: Out and about, engaging with the world

Session aim: Use Welsh in simple real-life moments when you are out and about.

How we will achieve this: Practical phrases, role-play options, and tiny “try it if you fancy it” tasks.

What we will cover: Signs, directions basics, greetings in public places, and small confidence-building interactions.

Outcome: You will have a few Welsh phrases you can try in the real world without pressure.

How to use this page: Use arrows or step buttons. If your time or energy is low, do Get Ready only.

Step 1: Get Ready

Spot one Welsh word on a sign or poster this week. Take a photo if you want.

Step 2: More Get Ready

Practise one “polite” phrase quietly at home. You are just getting your mouth used to it.

Step 3: Live session

We will practise simple out-and-about scenarios. You can speak, whisper, or just follow along.

Step 4: After the session

Try one phrase in the real world, if it feels safe. If not, share your sign photo or one word in WhatsApp instead.

Step 5: Curiosity thread

Pick a Welsh place-name you pass often. Look up what it means and share your favourite part of it.
