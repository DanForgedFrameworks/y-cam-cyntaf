Session 5: The Grounded Café Experience

Session aim: To manage simple transactions or social connection to unlock the course reward.

Key topics: Coffi, Te, Cacen, Bisged, Cawl, Tost

Bloom’s outcome: Demonstrate the ability to order a snack or drink or converse briefly.

How to use this page: You can keep this simple. Get Ready is enough if you are tired.

Session steps

Get Ready

Watch: “The Coffee and Cake Run” scenario video.

Task: Self-checklist: “I can order one drink and one snack”.

Live Session

Theme: The Pop-Up Café (60–90 mins)

  • Mentimeter: “What is the best biscuit?”
  • Role-play ordering with kind, low-pressure practice.
  • Short phrases for polite conversation.

After Session

Task: Go to Grounded and use any Welsh (order a coffee or say “Bore da, sut wyt ti?”). If you cannot go, post a pretend order in WhatsApp.

Curiosity Thread

Optional: Café culture, food names, and everyday Welsh words you might notice out and about.

Purpose: spark interest and conversation, not testing.
