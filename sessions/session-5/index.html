Session 5: Shopping and simple transactions

Aim: Feel more confident using Welsh in simple shopping or ordering situations.

How we’ll achieve this: Short phrases, friendly role-play, and repetition that feels safe.

What we’ll cover: Ordering politely, simple amounts, “please/thank you”, and common shop words.

Outcome: You can do a small real-world interaction without getting stuck.

How to use this page: Use the arrows to move through the steps. If you are low on time or energy, do Get Ready only.

Step 1: Get Ready

  • Plis (please)
  • Diolch (thank you)
  • Ga i …? (Can I have …?)
  • Faint yw e? (How much is it?)

Step 2: Get Ready (optional)

Mini practice: Build one request.

  • Ga i goffi, plis? (Can I have coffee, please?)
  • Ga i de, plis? (Can I have tea, please?)

Step 3: Live session

  • Polite phrases and simple questions
  • Role-play: ordering something small
  • Confidence practice: “good enough Welsh”

Step 4: After the session

Small follow-on: Practise one “Ga i …?” request at home (out loud or quietly).
