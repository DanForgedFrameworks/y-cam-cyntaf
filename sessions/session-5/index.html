Session 5: Retail and ordering

How to use this page: Use the arrows to move through the steps. If your time or energy is low, do Get Ready only. That is enough.

Session aim: Build confidence using Welsh in shops and simple ordering situations.
How we will achieve this: Short phrases, role-play, and friendly practice with common retail interactions.
What we will cover: Retail interactions, ordering, and simple polite phrases.
Outcome: You will be able to try small bits of Welsh when buying something or ordering, without pressure.
Step

Get Ready

Pick one polite phrase you can use in a shop. Practise it once. That is enough.

Live session

Try simple ordering and retail scenarios in a supportive way.

After the session

If you feel up for it, try one phrase in real life. If not, a quick recap is still a win.

Curiosity thread

Optional: find a Welsh word on packaging or in a shop and share it with the group.
