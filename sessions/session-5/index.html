Session 5: Retail and ordering interactions

Session aim: Feel more confident using Welsh in simple shop or café moments.

How we will achieve this: Short repeatable phrases, friendly role-play options, and low-pressure practice.

What we will cover: Polite requests, “please and thank you”, ordering basics, and simple responses.

Outcome: You will be able to try a small Welsh interaction in a shop or café, without needing to be perfect.

How to use this page: Use arrows or step buttons. If your time or energy is low, do Get Ready only.

Step 1: Get Ready

Choose one phrase you would actually use (even if it is just “diolch”). That is your prep.

Step 2: More Get Ready

Practise saying it once or twice. Quiet practice counts. You do not have to perform.

Step 3: Live session

We will practise short ordering-style phrases with lots of support and plenty of humour.

Step 4: After the session

If you want, try one phrase in the real world. If not, just share which phrase you chose in WhatsApp.

Step 5: Curiosity thread

Find a Welsh food word or menu item. Bring it back and tell the group which one you would order.
