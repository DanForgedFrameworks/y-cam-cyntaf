Session 5: Ordering and everyday interactions

Session aim: Feel comfortable using Welsh for a simple order or everyday interaction.

How we’ll achieve this: We’ll practise short, useful phrases and role-play in a friendly way so it feels real, not like a test.

What we’ll cover: ordering basics, simple polite phrases, and short social interactions.

Outcome: You’ll be able to make a simple order (or start a short interaction) using Welsh in a way that feels achievable.

How to use this page: Use the arrows to move through the steps for this session. We understand you are doing this in your own time, so engage with the resources as much as you can. They are here to help you and your peers get the most from the live session. If your time or energy is low, do Get Ready only, so everyone arrives ready. Even if you are not all on the same page, you will be in the same book.

Session steps

Get Ready

Time: 10 minutes.

  • Watch a short clip about ordering and everyday phrases.
  • Choose one phrase you can use (one is enough).

Live Session

Time: 60 to 90 minutes.

  • Warm-up: polite phrases together.
  • Role-play: ordering in a friendly, no-pressure way.
  • Optional extension: short social phrases.

After Session

Time: 5 minutes.

  • If you can, try one phrase in real life.
  • If you cannot, write your “pretend order” in the group.

Curiosity Thread

Optional: 5 to 15 minutes.

  • Explore Welsh food and drink words.
  • Share one you like, or one you spotted.
