Session 2: Basic introductions and interactions

How to use this page: Use the arrows to move through the steps. If your time or energy is low, do Get Ready only. That is enough.

Session aim: Build confidence introducing yourself and responding in simple ways.
How we will achieve this: Short, repeatable phrases, lots of friendly practice, and pair or small-group support.
What we will cover: Basic introductions, simple questions and answers, and easy conversation starters.
Outcome: You will be able to start and keep a short Welsh interaction going without feeling overwhelmed.
