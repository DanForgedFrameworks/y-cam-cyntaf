Session 2: Basic introductions and interactions

Session aim: Feel comfortable greeting people and introducing yourself in a simple way.

How we’ll achieve this: We’ll practise short, repeatable phrases, and use friendly pair or group activities so you can try things out safely.

What we’ll cover: greetings, simple introductions, basic replies, and keeping a short conversation going.

Outcome: You’ll be able to introduce yourself and respond to others with a few simple Welsh phrases.

How to use this page: Use the arrows to move through the steps for this session. We understand you are doing this in your own time, so engage with the resources as much as you can. They are here to help you and your peers get the most from the live session. If your time or energy is low, do Get Ready only, so everyone arrives ready. Even if you are not all on the same page, you will be in the same book.

Session steps

Get Ready

Time: 10 minutes.

  • Watch a short clip on greetings and introductions.
  • Pick one greeting you like and one “my name is…” phrase.

Live Session

Time: 60 to 90 minutes.

  • Warm-up: easy greetings as a group.
  • Pair practice: introduce yourself and ask one simple question.
  • Group activity: short, supportive conversation loops.

After Session

Time: 5 minutes.

  • Post a greeting in the group.
  • Reply to one other person.

Curiosity Thread

Optional: 5 to 15 minutes.

  • Notice Welsh names or words in your area.
  • Share one you spotted or liked.
