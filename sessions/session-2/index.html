Session 2: Connection and Digital Interaction

Session aim: To confidently introduce oneself and manage a digital text conversation.

Key topics: Introductions, simple conversation, WhatsApp interaction

Bloom’s outcome: Apply greetings in a text-based format and respond to a peer.

How to use this page: Use the arrows to move through the steps. If time is tight, focus on Get Ready.

Session steps

Get Ready

Watch: “Meeting New People” video.

Task: Script your own three-sentence introduction (name, where you are from, one thing you like).

Live Session

Theme: The Social Circle (60–90 mins)

  • Activity: “Speed Friending” (breakout rooms or pairs).
  • Practise greetings and short introductions.
  • Simple text conversation patterns for WhatsApp.

After Session

Task: The WhatsApp Check-in. Send a greeting to the group. Wait for a peer to respond, then reply back.

Curiosity Thread

Optional: Signposting to everyday Welsh names, nicknames, or common phrases you may notice locally.

Purpose: a friendly conversation starter, not homework.
