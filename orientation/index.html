What this course is

Y Cam Cyntaf is a friendly first step into Welsh. We focus on small wins, confidence, and using Welsh in real life.

  • Short pre-work before each session
  • Live practice with your group
  • Light follow-up to help it stick

How sessions work

Each session page follows the same pattern:

  1. Pre-work (short and simple)
  2. Live session focus (what we practise together)
  3. After session (a 5–10 minute task or social prompt)
  4. Curiosity Thread (optional extra)

Curiosity Threads (optional)

These are optional stretch targets. They are there to make things interesting and support wellbeing through connection.

What they might include:

  • A short link to a Welsh place, story, myth, or nature topic
  • A mini prompt you can talk about with others (in Welsh or English)
  • A quick activity (for example, a short poll or reflection)

If you skip these, you are still fully on track.

Support and contact

If you are stuck or feeling overwhelmed, you do not need to push through alone.

  • Speak to your facilitator during the session
  • Use the group support route you have been given (for example, WhatsApp)
  • Contact Tidy Butt via the usual charity contact routes
