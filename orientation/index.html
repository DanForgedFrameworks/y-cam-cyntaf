Orientation

A quick overview of how the course works, what to expect, and how to use this hub between sessions. Keep it low pressure. Do what you can. If your time or energy is low, focus on Get Ready for your next session and you will still be able to take part.

Suggested approach

  • Use Get Ready if you are short on time or energy.
  • Use the hub to find your next session and download print-friendly materials.
  • Use Support and contact if you need help or something is not working.
