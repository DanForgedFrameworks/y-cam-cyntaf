Orientation

Purpose: To welcome you, reduce anxiety, and explain how Y Cam Cyntaf works before any session-specific learning begins.

What to expect

  • A friendly welcome video (from the Tidy Butt founder).
  • A plain-language overview of what Y Cam Cyntaf is and is not.
  • How learning will happen: small steps, listening, noticing, trying.
  • Reassurance about pressure, mistakes, and participation.
  • How to use the digital resources and print-ready backups.
  • An introduction to optional Curiosity Threads.

Outcome: You feel safe, informed, and confident enough to take a first step.
