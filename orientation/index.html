How this course works

These resources help you get the most from the live sessions and support shared learning in the room. If you can, try to engage with the steps before each session so you and your peers can get more from the activities together.

If your time or energy is low, focus on Get Ready only. That is enough to help you arrive feeling more confident and connected.

When you are ready, head to Session 1 and complete the Get Ready activities. If your time or energy is low, doing just that is more than enough to get started.
