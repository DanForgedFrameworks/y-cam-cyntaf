What this course is

Y Cam Cyntaf is a friendly first step into Welsh. We focus on small wins, confidence, and using Welsh in real life.

  • Short pre-work before each session
  • Live practice with your group
  • Light follow-up to help it stick

How sessions work

  1. Pre-work (short and simple)
  2. Live session focus (what we practise together)
  3. After session (a 5–10 minute task or social prompt)
  4. Curiosity Thread (optional extra)

Curiosity Threads (optional)

Optional stretch targets to keep things interesting and support wellbeing through connection.

  • A short link to a Welsh place, story, myth, or nature topic
  • A mini prompt you can discuss with others
  • A quick optional activity (poll or reflection)

If you skip these, you are still fully on track.
