How this course works

Tip: Hover over a card to reveal the details. On mobile, tap a card to reveal the bullet points.

What Y Cam Cyntaf is (and is not)

A quick, plain-language overview.

  • A gentle first step into Welsh, with friendly support from Tidy Butt and the group.
  • A space to listen, notice, try a few words, and celebrate small wins together.
  • Not an exam course or a “perfect grammar” class. Mistakes are expected and welcome.

How learning will happen

Short steps, friendly practice.

  • Short digital “Get Ready” activities between sessions.
  • Low-pressure live sessions with games, stories, and wellbeing check-ins.
  • Light, social practice you can try in day-to-day life if you have the energy.

Pressure, mistakes, and participation

You choose your pace.

  • You choose how much you join in on any given day.
  • You can listen quietly, speak when you feel ready, or just try one word.
  • Mistakes are treated as part of learning, and often part of the laughter.

Digital resources

What this hub is for.

  • This hub helps you get the most from each live session.
  • Each session follows a simple structure: Get Ready, Live session, After the session, Curiosity thread.
  • Use it on your phone or laptop, whenever it suits your week.

Offline backups

If printing helps, that is fine.

  • You can print pages as PDFs using your browser (Print, Save as PDF).
  • The Downloads area will grow with printable handouts and session summaries.
  • Offline options are there to reduce stress, not add homework.

Curiosity threads

Optional stretch targets.

  • Optional “rabbit holes” linked to Welsh nature, place-names, stories, or culture.
  • No expectation to finish them. They are there to spark interest and give you something fun to share.
  • You will see them labelled as Curiosity thread in the session cards.

When you are ready

These resources are here to help you get the most from the live sessions and to support shared learning in the room. They help everyone arrive prepared, so if you are not on the same page, you are at least in the same book.

Aim to engage with the resources as much as you can. They benefit you and your peers, and they help the live sessions feel more comfortable and more connected.

If your time or energy is low, just complete the Get Ready steps for Session 1. That is more than enough to get started.
